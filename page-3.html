Here goes the explanation about the job

Between 2019 and 2023 I was an on-air personality at the KVCU radio station. I hosted a music show, had guests, hosted talk show segments, edited and created buffers, and took calls. Additionally, I served as an Assistant Music Director listening to and reviewing new records labels would send us for rotation. I also assisted in planning community outreach events. Here are some playlist examples as well as a picture of the studio space.

-DJ music and operate soundboard live on air

-Taking live calls, writing and reading news bulletins

-Public outreaching including advertising live on air, and organizing group-community activities such as fundraisers.

-Listening, reviewing, and cataloging promotional records sent from labels.
